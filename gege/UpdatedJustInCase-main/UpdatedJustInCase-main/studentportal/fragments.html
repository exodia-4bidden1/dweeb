User Agreement

Last Updated: May 10, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Campus Device Lending Program (“Program”) operated by University Technology Services. This User Agreement (“Agreement”) governs your use of our device lending services and outlines your rights and responsibilities. By accessing or using our services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Agreement.

2. Eligibility

To be eligible to borrow devices through the Program, you must be:

  • A currently enrolled student, faculty, or staff member at the University
  • In good standing with the University and the Program
3. Borrowing Terms
  1. Loan Period: The standard loan period is 14 calendar days from the date of checkout, unless otherwise specified.
  2. Extensions: One extension may be granted for an additional 7 days, provided that:
    • The device is not reserved by another user
    • Your request is submitted at least 24 hours before the due date
    • You have no outstanding overdue devices or unpaid fees
  3. Limitations: You may borrow up to 2 devices simultaneously.
4. User Responsibilities

While a device is in your possession, you are responsible for:

  • Maintaining the device in good working condition
  • Protecting the device from theft, loss, or damage
  • Using the device for legal and appropriate purposes in accordance with University policies
  • Not installing unauthorized software or making hardware modifications
  • Backing up any personal data before returning (all data will be wiped upon return)
  • Reporting any problems, damage, or technical issues immediately
